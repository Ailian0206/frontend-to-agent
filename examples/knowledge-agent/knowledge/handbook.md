# Team Handbook

## Release cadence

The product release window is every Thursday at 16:00 Asia/Shanghai. A release
must have a green CI run and approval from one on-call engineer.

## Incident policy

Severity-one incidents require an incident channel within five minutes and a
written review within two working days. The review must focus on system causes,
not individual blame.

## Expense policy

Software expenses below CNY 500 can be approved by the team lead. Higher amounts
require cost-center owner approval before purchase.
